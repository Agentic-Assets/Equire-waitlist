## Core Capabilities

### Upload Documents. Get Answers Instantly.

Drop in a rent roll, an offering memorandum, or a Phase I ESA — EQUIRE reads the document, extracts every relevant data point, and maps it directly into your deal. Every extracted value carries its source, confidence score, and document attribution so you always know where a number came from.

**10 document types recognized automatically.** Rent rolls, T-12 operating statements, lease agreements, title commitments, ALTA surveys, appraisals, PCAs, environmental reports, offering memorandums, and zoning reports. PDF, Word, and Excel supported.

### Build Institutional-Grade DCF Models — Without a Spreadsheet

EQUIRE's valuation engine builds fully underwritten discounted cash flow models with real amortization schedules, tenant-level cash flow projections, and levered and unlevered returns.

- **25 configurable assumptions** with smart defaults pulled from your fund parameters, documents, and market research
- **Scenario analysis** across base, upside, and downside cases
- **Sensitivity tables** on any one or two variables — IRR and equity multiple across the full range
- **Live metrics**: IRR, equity multiple, going-in cap rate, cash-on-cash, DSCR, debt yield, NPV, and DCF indicated value

No formulas to audit. No circular references to untangle. Adjust an assumption and watch the entire model recalculate in real time.

### A 6-Step Guided Valuation Wizard

Not sure where to start? EQUIRE's valuation wizard walks your team through the entire model-building process:

1. **Verify Property Details** — confirm what we extracted from your documents
2. **Set Investment Parameters** — purchase price, financing, hold period
3. **Gap Analysis** — see what data we have and what's missing
4. **Market Research** — AI-powered web research fills gaps with cited market data
5. **Risk Assessment** — flag assumptions that need attention
6. **Build Model** — one click to generate a fully populated DCF

### IC Memos That Write Themselves

EQUIRE generates a complete 12-section Investment Committee memorandum — the kind your IC actually wants to read. Every section pulls directly from your deal data, extracted documents, market research, and team inputs.

**Executive Summary** | **Transaction Overview** | **Investment Thesis** | **Market Analysis** | **Property & Physical Condition** | **Tenancy & Lease Analysis** | **Financial Analysis** | **Financing Structure** | **Risk Factors & Mitigants** | **Operational Plan** | **Fund & Investor Considerations** | **Proposed Resolution**

Export as a polished Word document with cover page, table of contents, inline charts, source citations, and institutional formatting — ready for your investment committee.

### Due Diligence That Tracks Itself

EQUIRE monitors your document extraction in real time and automatically generates due diligence action items when it finds something that needs attention.

- Environmental RECs identified? **Flagged as critical.**
- PCA shows immediate repairs exceeding $500K? **Added to the checklist with the dollar amount.**
- Roof condition rated poor? **Escalated automatically.**

22 default checklist items across 6 categories — Documents, Legal, Financial, Physical, Environmental, and Title — plus auto-generated items triggered by what's actually in your deal documents.

### AI That Understands Commercial Real Estate

EQUIRE's AI assistant isn't a generic chatbot. It's a purpose-built analyst that understands your deal inside and out.

**Ask it anything:**
- *"What are the key risks on this deal?"*
- *"Run a sensitivity on exit cap rate and rent growth."*

It knows which page you're on, which deal you're looking at, and what data is available. It can update your model, search your documents, pull market data, resolve extraction conflicts, and manage your rent roll — all through natural conversation.

**17 specialized tools** including document management, valuation modeling, market intelligence for 100+ U.S. metros, web research with citations, and portfolio analytics.

Access real-time metro-level data for every major U.S. market — job growth, unemployment, building permits, housing prices, GDP, migration trends, and more. Compare markets side by side. Rank them by any metric. Pull national macro data including Treasury yields, CPI, and GDP growth.

No separate subscriptions. No manual data gathering. The data your team needs to underwrite a market is already there.
